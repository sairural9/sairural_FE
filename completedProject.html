Health and Safety Farmer Awareness Programme

Funder Name: Syngenta

Implementing Agencies: IRFT and Sai Rural Development Trust

We have trained 5000 Farmers and 500 Spray-men from Maheshwar and Bhagwanpura blocks of Khargone District, MP on Farmers Health and Safety while using and handling agro chemicals followed by Usage of Syngenta PPE kit and Demonstration.

  • A 50 Days Van Campaign was organized to reach maximum number of farmers/villagers.
  • A Van was loaded with an audio system and decorated with the Banners of safety measures.

Regenerative Agriculture Practices

Funder Name: Syngenta

Implementing Agencies: IRFT & Sai Rural Development Trust

Growers Training on Regenerative Agriculture Practices in Vegetable field production.

We have trained around 1000 growers from Maharashtra, Gujarat & Karnataka.

  • Key Principles and Practices:
  • Minimized soil disturbance.
  • Plants in the ground year round.
  • Diversified crops in time and space.
  • Precision application of biological and chemical inputs.
  • Integrated livestock when possible.

About Us
